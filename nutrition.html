Nutrition

The image below is called a food pyramid

The food Pyramid can give you an idea of what to eat and how much of it you should eat or do, as the triangle get smaller the less you should use/consume.

Here are a list of macro nutrients you should take into consideration if you want to have a successful diet

  • carbohydrates:

    • carbs give you quick energy, when you eat carbs you turn them into glucose or sugars

    Recommenced in take: 4 calories per gram.

  • protein

    • protein is used for muscle development this macronutrient is key to gain maximum amounts of muscle

    Recommenced in take: 4 calories per gram.

  • fats

    • fats are stored energy that can be use as a reserve for intense work outs it is not recommend to over consume this macro nutrient, because it will lead to weight gain(not the good kind)

    Recommenced in take: 9 calories per gram.
